Famous Temples

Ratanpur Mahamaya

The right hand of Goddess Sati was dropped in Ratanpur. Lord Shiva gave himself the name of the Kumbari Shakti Peeth by being self-realizede,Bilaspur,Chhattisgarh

saravmangala Mandir

The Goddess of this temple is Durga. This temple was built by the ancestors of Rajeshwar Dayal one of the Zamindar in Korba,Chhattisgarh

Jatmai Mata Mandir

Jatmai Mata Mandir or Jatmayi Temple is a Hindu temple of Goddess Durga ji located in Gariaband district 80 km from state capital Raipur, Chhattisgarh.

Bhoramdev Temple

The Bhoramdeo Temple draws similarities to the Konark Temple and the famous Khajuraho Temples located in Kawardha,Chhattisgarh.

Chandrahasini Temple

It is said that Goddess Chandrahasini Devi appeared here in front of "King Chandrahas" of ancient Chandrapur,janjgir champa,Chhattisgarh.

Maa Bamleshwari Temple

Legend of the Bamleshwari Devi Temple: It is believed that some 2200 years ago, Dongargarh was known as Kamakhya Nagri and was ruled by King Veersen who was childless,located in Dongargarh,Chhattisgarh.

Mata Vindhyavashini Mandir

The Vindhyavasini and Angarmoti temples are famous for their art and cultural beliefs, followed by many people of Dhamtari,Chhattisgarh.

Patal Bhairvi Temple

Maa Patal Bhairavi Rajnandgaon temple is a place of divine worship where devotees can experience the presence of Maa Bhairavi, a form of Goddess Kali,Chhattisgarh.
