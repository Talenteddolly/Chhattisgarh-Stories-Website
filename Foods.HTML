Foods of Chhattisgarh


Muthia

Muthia are dumplings cooked in the traditional style.

Aamat

Aamat is considered as the Sambhar of Bastar region of the state.

Bhajia

Bhajia is a famous street food of Chhattisgarh.

Tilgur

Roasted sesame seeds mixed with jaggery and peanuts.

Chila

Chila is an important part of breakfast in Chhattisgarh made with rice flour.

Sabudana ki Kichdi

Sabudana ki Kichdi is prepared with soaked Sabudana balls.

Fara

Rice Flour and Rice are combined and then steamed and pan fried.

Thethri

This savory delicacy is crafted from besan (gram flour) infused with aromatic spices.
